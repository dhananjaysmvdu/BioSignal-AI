About Ethical Compliance

Bias Score (BS): Measures group disparity as |max(group_mean) - min(group_mean)| / mean(global). Lower is better (BS < 0.05 = acceptable).

Fairness Score: Computed as 1 - BS. Higher indicates better parity across demographic groups. Threshold: 0.95 (95% fairness minimum).

Escalation Policy: If fairness < threshold or BS > 0.10, system triggers audit + notify (logged to decision trace).

Data Sources: observatory/fairness_metrics.csv (accuracy by group), reports/ethics/ (compliance history).
